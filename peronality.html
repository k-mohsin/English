Personality Development

By Khan Mohsin

Personality is a combination of various positive and negative characteristics that form an individual's unique character. Developing your personality starts with understanding yourself. In this blog, we will discuss some easy ways to develop your personality and become a better and much-evolved person.

Evaluate yourself – To evaluate yourself, identify and make a list of your positive as well as negative traits. This will help you to understand your strengths and shortcomings.
Focus on your positive traits – Once you have identified your positive traits, it is now time to focus on strengthening and sharpening them. This will help you gain confidence.
Work on your short-comings – Now that you’re confident with your positive traits, you must also work on eliminating your negative traits that form a core of your weakness. This could be done by employing alternative ways to respond to a specific situation, by being patient and also by speaking up about your problems to friends, relatives and counsellors.
